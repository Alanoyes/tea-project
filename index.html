About Mandala Teas

We are proud to provide you with the best customer service experience possible. We were founded on the premise that everybody deserves access to high quality teas that are grown with good agricultural practices.
Many of our teas are certified organic and the teas which we provide which are not certified are grown by farmers which we have personally met and assured that they are grown in a manner which is up to our standards.
